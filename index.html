No greater love for the water

My first time taking on the Sandy River was the most thrilling of my life. With just myself, two friends, and a heavily patched rubber raft with smoothed branches for ‘oars’, we three set out to explore places we had never seen that were nevertheless close to home. The joy of discovery, the rush of adrenaline, and the satisfaction of conquest over the white peaks where the river ran roughest is an experience never forgotten.

Sharing the experience

This is the kind of experience we wanted everyone to have the chance to feel, and so we three friends created the Rubber Branch Whitewater Rafting Company. We have been sharing the joys of challenging the river for 8 years and it is time to expand our influence to the world! (And bring in more tourists, that never hurts!)

From the rookie to the veteran, we have you covered!

The Rubber Branch Whitewater Rafting Company provides everything you need for the river; from classes for rookies to the best gear for veterans. Experienced guides provide customers with rafting locations full of beauty, history, and challenge (to varying degrees) to satisfy anyone with a thirst for thrills and no adversion to getting wet! We can satisfy any duck-footed river rafter and in no time set them ready to conquer their next waterway!
